| < 4.0   | :x:                |

## Reporting a Vulnerability

Report vulnerabilities privately by emailing:
L00181440@atu.ie

Use this section to tell people how to report a vulnerability.

To help us triage and respond quickly, please include:

A description of the issue

Steps to reproduce

Potential impact

Any proof-of-concept code (if applicable)

Your recommended fix or mitigation (optional)

We aim to acknowledge reports within 48 hours.

You can expect an initial assessment within 5 business days.

We will provide updates at least every 7 days until the issue is resolved.

Once fixed, we will coordinate a responsible disclosure timeline with you.
